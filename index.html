UI/UX Case Study

Purple Pages Library Platform

Problem: Users often struggle to find and access books quickly due to cluttered navigation and overwhelming interfaces on existing library platforms.

Goal: To design a simple, user-friendly interface that allows users to easily browse, search, and access a wide variety of books.

Solution:

  • Clean, intuitive dashboard highlighting featured and recent books
  • Easy search and filter options for users to find books quickly
  • Consistent color scheme inspired by “Purple Pages” branding
  • Responsive design for desktop and mobile devices

Result: Users can now browse and access books efficiently, improving engagement and overall satisfaction.
